Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What is your design process?

Our design process begins with an initial consultation to understand your vision, needs, and budget. We then create a concept design, develop detailed plans, select materials and furnishings, and finally oversee the implementation to ensure everything meets our high standards.

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on the scope and complexity of each project. We offer flexible packages including hourly consultations, flat-fee design services, and full project management. Contact us for a personalized quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you work with specific budgets?

Yes, we work with clients across various budget ranges. Our team is skilled at maximizing value and finding creative solutions that deliver exceptional results within your financial parameters. We believe great design should be accessible.

What areas do you serve?

Based in Kadur, we serve clients throughout Karnataka and surrounding regions. We also offer virtual design consultations for clients outside our immediate area, allowing us to work with clients across India.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A single room design typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complete home renovations may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you handle procurement and installation?

Yes, we offer comprehensive services including furniture procurement, material sourcing, and project management. Our team coordinates with trusted vendors and contractors to ensure seamless execution of your design vision.
